How to Order

Follow these simple steps to place your order

How it works
1
Enter your table number
On the welcome screen, type your table number and tap Proceed. You'll find your table number on the table card.
2
Choose Veg or Non-Veg
Select your preferred category — Veg or Non-Veg. You can switch between them anytime using the tabs at the top.
3
Add items to cart
Tap any dish to open it. Set the quantity using + and − buttons, then tap Confirm Order to add it to your cart.
4
Add drinks if you like
Tap the 🥤 Drinks button at the bottom to browse and add drinks to your cart alongside your food.
5
Place order & pay
Go to 🛒 Cart, review your items, tap Place Order and then PAY NOW to complete payment via UPI.
